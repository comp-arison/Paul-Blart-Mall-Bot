Paul Blart: Mall Bot

Command List

Main Commands

Command Name Description Usage
help I show you this list of commands. $help {command}
hello I say hi back. $hello
quote I say a wacky quote from one of my movies. Put a number after that and I'll say that quote from the server's quote list. $quote {number}
quotelist I give you a list of all the quotes the server has unlocked. $quotelist$quotes
resetquotes I reset the quote list for the server. You need to be an adminstrator for this. $resetquotes
quiz Test your knowledge of Paul Blart quotes. $quiz
blartify Say something and I'll Blartify it. $blartify [text]
watch I give you links to watch my movies. $watch
arrest Say a name and I'll arrest 'em. $arrest [user]
trivia Test your knowledge of the Paul Blart universe. $trivia
citation Prints out a citation to those evil doers. $citation [Written to]; [Reason]; [Penalty]
meme Generates a dvd cover with the text you say. $meme [text]

Stock Market

Command Name Description Usage
stock Gives you the latest information on the value of Blartcoins. $stock$stocks
buy Purchaces Blartcoins for the given price. (You can buy them even if you don't have enough money.) $buy [ammount]
sell Sells your Blartcoins at the given price. $sell [ammount]
mine Solve for X, get a Blartcoin! $mine
give Gives Blartcoins to the person you specify. $give [user] [ammount]
bankruptcy Resets your balance. I'll ask for confirmation. $quiz
balance Tells you how many Blartcoins and how much money you have. $balance$bal
leaderboard See who is better than you. $leaderboard$lb
